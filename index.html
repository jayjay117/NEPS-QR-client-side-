About NEPS-QR

Nigeria's innovative cashless payment solution

What is NEPS-QR?

NEPS-QR (NQR) is a cashless payment system that allows users to pay registered businesses by scanning their unique QR codes, eliminating the need for bank app transfers or POS terminals.

Developed by Innovative Technologies, NQR is inspired by Nigeria's actual NQR standard but designed as a lightweight, efficient payment solution for everyday transactions.
